Important Notice: PerlerBeads Designer is a completely offline application. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal information or data.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any data.

PerlerBeads Designer is a completely offline application. All features run locally on your device. We do not:

  • Collect your personal information
  • Collect your project data
  • Collect your usage habits or behavioral data
  • Use any analytics tools or tracking technologies
  • Share data with any third parties

2. Data Storage

All your data (including projects, settings, etc.) is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

  • Project Data: Stored in your device's local file system
  • App Settings: Stored in your device's local settings
  • Image Data: Only accessed when you grant permission, never uploaded or shared

3. Permission Usage

The app only requests the following permissions when necessary:

  • Photo Library Access: Used to convert photos into perler bead design patterns, only when you actively choose an image
  • Photo Library Save: Used to save exported perler bead design images to your photo library, only when you actively export

These permissions are only used for features you actively use. We do not access your photo library in the background or upload any content.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not include any third-party analytics services, advertising services, or data collection services.

In-app purchase functionality uses Apple's StoreKit framework. All purchase processes are handled by Apple, and we do not obtain or store your payment information.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any data, we do not collect children's personal information.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, data security is entirely protected by your device's security measures (such as passwords, Face ID, Touch ID, etc.).

We recommend that you:

  • Regularly back up your device
  • Use your device's security features to protect your data
  • Do not lend your device to others (if it contains important projects)

7. Privacy Policy Changes

If we need to modify this privacy policy in the future, we will notify you when the app is updated. Since we do not collect data, changes to the privacy policy will not affect your stored data.
