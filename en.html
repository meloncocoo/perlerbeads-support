Q: How do I export my project?

A: In the editor, tap the share button in the top right corner, then select "Export Image" to save a high-resolution image to your photo library.

Q: How do I convert a photo to a perler bead pattern?

A: On the main screen, tap "Create from Image", select your photo, adjust the crop area if needed, and the app will automatically match colors and generate a perler bead pattern.

Q: How do I manage my projects?

A: On the main screen's project list, you can browse, search, rename, or delete projects. All projects are automatically saved.

Q: What grid sizes are supported?

A: Supports grid sizes from 5x5 to 102x102, meeting the needs of different sized perler bead projects.

Q: How do I undo an operation?

A: In the editor, you can use the Undo/Redo feature to undo or redo operations, making your design process more flexible.

Q: Can I use the eraser tool?

A: Yes, the app includes an eraser function that allows you to easily modify your design by removing beads.

Q: Does the app support multiple languages?

A: Yes, the app supports both Chinese and English. You can change the language in your device settings.
